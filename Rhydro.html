Hydro Energy Systems in Tamil Nadu

Conventional Hydropower

Traditional dam-based systems utilizing river flow:

  • Mettur Dam (120 MW capacity)
  • Pykara Hydroelectric Project (150 MW)
  • Average 3,500 MU annual generation

Pumped Storage Systems

Energy storage solution using surplus power:

  • Upper reservoir (Kalvarayan Hills)
  • Lower reservoir (Existing dams)
  • 80% round-trip efficiency

Small Hydropower

Decentralized rural energy projects:

  • 100kW-25MW capacity range
  • 47 operational plants statewide
  • New 2024 policy incentives
